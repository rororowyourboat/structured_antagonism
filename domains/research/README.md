# Research Domain

SA instruments for designing, auditing, and synthesizing research studies and experiments.

## Status: Planned

This domain is under construction. The instruments will follow the same Design → Audit → Synthesize loop, adapted for the research epistemic dependency chain:

```
Layer 1: Question & Constructs  — What hypothesis? What constructs? What's the theory?
Layer 2: Measurement & Design   — How measured? What design? What controls?
Layer 3: Procedure & Flows      — What protocol? What sequence? What decision rules?
Layer 4: Validity & Power       — What threats? What power? What sensitivity?
Layer 5: Reporting & Replication — How reported? How replicated? What archival?
```

## Four People

| Role | In this domain |
|------|---------------|
| Domain expert | Methodologist (experimental design theory) |
| Protocol designer | Study protocol designer |
| Modeler | Researcher executing the study |
| Consumer | Reader, reviewer, or policy-maker |

## Planned Instruments

| Instrument | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| `experiment-design.md` | Generate a structured experiment/study design |
| `design-audit.md` | Adversarially review an experimental design |
| `design-synthesize.md` | Full audit-synthesize loop with validity and power checks |

See the [construction guide](../../docs/construction-guide.md) for how these will be built.
